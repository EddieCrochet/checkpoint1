"I like Dinner Party, because it says exactly what it is while keeping a nostalgic feel for more traditional dinner parties that will appeal to the target millenial user base" -Steven Protsko, Dinner Party idea Founder © 2018

Tired of cooking for one or two people with dishes you are excited about, and having to constantly try and scale down a recipie? Well, now there's Dinner Party!

Dinner Party is both an app and an online platform that connects you with your neighbors in order to create an optimal dining experience, striving toward diversity.
